Rays of Hope

Heartfelt 2D Animation | Narrative & Visual Storytelling

Short Film: Rays of Hope | Narrative Animation

Visual Narrative & Style

Rays of Hope is a poignant 2D animated short film telling the story of a young girl escaping the devastating horrors of war. Struggling to survive in a vast, unforgiving desert, she faces countless hardships that test her resilience. Her journey takes a definitive turn when a kind-hearted soul from a distant oasis rescues her, offering not just a safe haven, but a genuine chance to begin a new life filled with hope and light.

Production & Visual Strategy

Storyboarding

Mapped out key emotional beats to ensure the 2-minute duration maintains a strong narrative arc and pacing.

Character Design

Designed the protagonist with oversized eyes to enhance the "innocence lost" theme and evoke stronger empathy from the audience.

Lighting & Shading

Used dynamic shadows in the desert scenes to emphasize the oppressive heat and the character's vulnerability.

Sound Scaping

Layered ambient wind noises with a minimalist piano score to heighten the sense of isolation before the climactic rescue.

Celestial Heroes

Epic 2D Animation | Action, Sacrifice & Environmental Storytelling

Short Film: Celestial Heroes | Action, Sacrifice & Environmental Storytelling

Visual Narrative & Style

A high-stakes 2D animated epic that chronicles the heroic stand of two legendary warriors against a cataclysmic meteorite threat. Celestial Heroes chronicles the heroic stand of two legendary warriors against monstrous creatures unleashed by a cataclysmic meteorite crash. Fighting with unwavering bravery to shield humanity, the warriors ultimately make the ultimate sacrifice to ensure a future for their people. A century later, the story comes full circle as their descendants return to the hallowed ground to honor the fallen heroes whose courage bought them an era of lasting peace.

Production & Visual Strategy

Environmental Storytelling & Temporal Shift

I utilized the environment as a silent narrator. By evolving the landscape from a chaotic, war-torn crash site to a serene place of remembrance, I visually mirrored the passage of a century and the transition from conflict to legacy.

Atmospheric Contrast & Color Theory

To heighten the emotional stakes, I developed a dual-palette system. High-intensity, chaotic tones were used for the battle scenes to evoke urgency, while ethereal, serene hues were reserved for the commemorative ending to symbolize lasting peace.

Advanced Diorama & Spatial Composition

Applying Diorama composition techniques, I created a sense of monumental scale. By layering foreground and background elements, I made the warriors appear heroic and small against the colossal scale of the meteorite threat, enhancing the sense of "sacrifice."

Dynamic Object Animation & Weight

Focused on the fluidity of environmental props and debris. By animating the meteorite impact with realistic weight and physics-based movement, I added a three-dimensional depth to the 2D medium, ensuring the combat felt grounded and impactful.

3D Animation

The Legacy To A True Man

Epic 2D Animation | Action, Sacrifice & Environmental Storytelling

Short Film: Celestial Heroes | Action, Sacrifice & Environmental Storytelling

Visual Narrative & Style

A 3D animated short film that follows a sheltered prince’s transition from royal protection to self-discovery. The story follows a young prince, sheltered by the walls of his kingdom and the overprotective gaze of the King and Queen. Driven by a longing for independence, he sneaks out to explore the world, only to find himself hunted by an elite assassin from a rival nation.

After being rescued by a skilled archer from a small town, the prince undergoes rigorous combat training. The film culminates in a high-stakes confrontation where the prince must prove his growth by defeating the assassin and claiming his own destiny.

Production Pipeline

End-to-End Pipeline Management

As the Project Leader, I orchestrated the entire animation workflow from initial storyboarding to final render. I ensured a cohesive visual style by integrating self-modeled 3D assets and managing task distribution to meet production milestones.

Technical Rigging & Character Animation

I personally developed the full-body character rigs from scratch. By focusing on weight distribution and secondary motion, I brought the prince’s transformation to life, ensuring his movements evolved from "unskilled royal" to "trained warrior" during the combat sequences.

Cinematic Storyboarding & Pacing

Translated the narrative into functional Animatics to establish the film’s emotional arc. I prioritized the timing of the final duel to ensure maximum dramatic impact, serving as the technical roadmap for the entire animation phase.

Asset Optimization & World-Building

Curated and optimized complex 3D environments from my modeling portfolio. I focused on asset performance during the integration phase to ensure smooth rendering without sacrificing the detailed aesthetic of the kingdom and the rival lands.
