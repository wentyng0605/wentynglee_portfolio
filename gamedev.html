The Fallen Olympus

A Mythological Action-Adventure Game developed using Unity Editor C#

The Fallen Olympus is a mythological action-adventure built in Unity, where players step into the role of Ares, the fallen God of War. Unbeknownst to Ares, he is a pawn in a grander scheme: Cronus, the Titan King, is secretly manipulating his journey to seize the Emblem’s power for his own dark ambitions.

As the Project Leader, I managed team coordination and streamlined the development workflow, ensuring a cohesive vision across all departments. My hands-on technical contributions included:

1. Gameplay Programming – Designed and implemented interactive puzzle and interaction mechanics.

2. UI Development – Created intuitive HUDs, menus, and UI interactions.

3. Environment Design – Built a immersive world-building and strategic gameplay elements.

The Solaria

A Dark Fantasy Action-Adventure Game developed using Unity Editor C#

On a mysterious, cursed island, once-human monsters roam, their bodies twisted by an ancient curse. Deep within this desolate land lies Solaria, a legendary flower rumored to possess the power to cure any illness.

To save his beloved, the protagonist embarks on a perilous journey to retrieve the flower. As players navigate this abandoned realm, they must unravel the island’s dark secrets while surviving the horrors that dwell within.

As the Project Leader, I managed the team’s development pipeline, ensuring high-quality execution from concept to final build. My primary technical focus was on System Architecture and Cinematic Immersion:

1. Puzzle & Interaction System –

  • Designed and programmed the core Interactive Puzzle System, creating logical challenges that integrate seamlessly with the environment.
  • Developed modular interaction scripts to allow for varied gameplay mechanics, from environmental manipulation to item-based progression.

    • 2. Dialogue System & Narrative UX –

  • Implemented a robust Dialogue System to facilitate character-driven storytelling.
  • Focused on the "Player-Narrative" experience, ensuring world-building elements were delivered naturally through NPC interactions and environmental cues.

    • 3. Cinematic & Cutscene Design –

  • Developed immersive in-game cinematics utilizing Unity’s Cinemachine and Timeline.
  • Choreographed camera movements and character staging to enhance emotional impact and provide a seamless transition between gameplay and storytelling.

    Zi Xuan In The Wonderland

    Mobile 3D Endless Runner Game developed using Unity Editor C#

    Trapped within a mystical dreamworld, Zi Xuan must navigate a surreal landscape to escape before time runs out. Players embark on a high-speed, visually immersive journey where they must run, dodge, and overcome obstacles. The game explores the theme of the subconscious, featuring ever-changing dreamscapes filled with both beauty and hidden dangers.

    As the solo developer of this project, I managed the entire lifecycle from conceptualization to deployment, focusing on performance optimization and user engagement:

    1. Gameplay Programming & Logic –

  • Developed the core Endless Runner mechanics, including procedural obstacle spawning and progressive difficulty scaling.
  • Engineered responsive player movement logic (swipe/tilt controls) to ensure precision in high-speed scenarios.

    • 2. Dreamlike Environment Design –

  • Built a 3D dreamscape that balances surreal aesthetics with visual clarity, ensuring that obstacles are easily distinguishable against the vibrant background.
  • Optimized environment assets to maintain a high frame rate, crucial for the fluid experience of a fast-paced runner.

    • 3. Mobile-First UI/UX Design –

  • Designed a mobile-friendly interface with a focus on minimalism, keeping the HUD unobtrusive to maximize the player’s field of view.
  • Refined the UX flow to ensure smooth transitions between game states (Menu -> Play -> Game Over -> Replay), reducing friction and increasing player retention.
