Game Design & Development

The Echoes of Elysium

A Mythological Action-Adventure Game developed using Unity Editor C#

How do you translate the abstract beauty of music into a tangible, navigable 3D world?

Elysium is an immersive VR experience where players traverse fragmented timelines to retrieve lost melodies. The core challenge was to design a seamless interaction model that feels natural in VR while maintaining a high level of narrative immersion.

Design Strategy & Technical Implementation

1. Immersive World-Building & Narrative Design – I crafted a mystery-driven storyline centered around the "City of Music." By utilizing environmental storytelling, I ensured that the architecture and lighting evolved as players restored harmony, providing immediate visual feedback on their progress.

2. Haptic-Centric VR Interaction – Developed core gameplay mechanics—including note collection and object manipulation—using physics-based interactions. I prioritized tactile feedback to reduce the "ghost hand" effect, making the retrieval of musical notes feel physically rewarding.

3. Comfort-Focused VR Locomotion – To combat VR motion sickness, I engineered a refined locomotion system that balances free movement with comfort vignettes. This ensures a smooth navigation experience through Elysium’s complex vertical architecture without breaking immersion.

4. Diegetic Dialogue & UI Systems – Instead of flat 2D menus, I implemented diegetic UI elements integrated directly into the VR environment. Character dialogues and task prompts were designed to exist within the world's space, adhering to HCI principles for spatial computing.

AR Business Card

A Dark Fantasy Action-Adventure Game developed using Unity Editor C#

On a mysterious, cursed island, once-human monsters roam, their bodies twisted by an ancient curse. Deep within this desolate land lies Solaria, a legendary flower rumored to possess the power to cure any illness.

To save his beloved, the protagonist embarks on a perilous journey to retrieve the flower. As players navigate this abandoned realm, they must unravel the island’s dark secrets while surviving the horrors that dwell within.

As the Project Leader, I managed the team’s development pipeline, ensuring high-quality execution from concept to final build. My primary technical focus was on System Architecture and Cinematic Immersion:

1. Puzzle & Interaction System –

  • Designed and programmed the core Interactive Puzzle System, creating logical challenges that integrate seamlessly with the environment.
  • Developed modular interaction scripts to allow for varied gameplay mechanics, from environmental manipulation to item-based progression.

    • 2. Dialogue System & Narrative UX –

  • Implemented a robust Dialogue System to facilitate character-driven storytelling.
  • Focused on the "Player-Narrative" experience, ensuring world-building elements were delivered naturally through NPC interactions and environmental cues.

    • 3. Cinematic & Cutscene Design –

  • Developed immersive in-game cinematics utilizing Unity’s Cinemachine and Timeline.
  • Choreographed camera movements and character staging to enhance emotional impact and provide a seamless transition between gameplay and storytelling.
